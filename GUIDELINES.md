# Guidelines for Contributing to `.HSX` Repository

Welcome! These guidelines are designed to ensure a safe, organized, and respectful environment for all contributors to the `.HSX` repository. Please read them carefully before submitting any contributions.

---

## 1. Follow the Rules
By contributing, you agree to:

- Follow **all rules outlined in our [Code of Conduct](./CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md)**.
- Respect the decisions of the repository maintainers.
- Keep discussions professional, constructive, and free of offensive language.

---

## 2. Copyright Compliance
All submissions must comply with copyright laws:

- Only submit extensions, code, or content that you **own** or have explicit permission to use.  
- Do **not submit content from third-party sources** without proper authorization.  
- Violating copyright terms may result in removal of submissions and further action.

---

## 4. Respect for the Maintainer
- This repository is managed by a **single developer**, so please be patient and respectful during the review process.  
- Excessive submissions or repeated rule violations may result in removal of your contributions or a warning email.
